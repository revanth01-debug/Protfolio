📊 Gender Distribution

Female customers account for 50.8% of transactions while male customers represent 49.2%. Both segments show similar spending patterns during Black Friday with women slightly leading in fashion categories and men in electronics.

💰 Spending Patterns by Age

Age group 26-35 shows the highest average transaction value at $9,247, followed by 36-45 age group at $8,891. Younger customers (18-25) tend to make more frequent but smaller purchases.

🏪 Store Performance
